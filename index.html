About Us

At Gatimaan creation, we believe in the unique blend of artistry and devotion. Our mission is not just to create idols, but to breathe life into them. Every single idol is crafted from the purest stone, reflecting years of experience and the unwavering dedication of our master artisans.

Our Artistic Process

The creation of each idol is a long and meticulous process that involves several stages:

  1. Stone Selection: Our work begins with the careful selection of the finest marble. We use various types of stones, including Vietnam White Marble, Makrana Marble, Ambaji Marble, Rajnagar Marble, Black Marble, and Red Marble. Each stone has its own unique beauty and character that shines through in the final piece.
  2. Block Cutting: The chosen stone is first cut into large blocks, sized according to the idol's specific dimensions.
  3. Sketching & Design: Our skilled artisans then use pencils to meticulously draw the idol's design onto the stone block, paying close attention to every intricate detail.
  4. Carving & Shaping: Once the sketch is complete, the carving process begins. With precision tools, the stone is slowly and carefully shaped to bring the design to life.
  5. Face Sculpting: The face is the most vital part of the idol. A specialized artist works on this stage to infuse the face with expression and a lifelike quality.
  6. Polishing: To give the idol a smooth and shiny finish, it is polished multiple times, bringing out the stone's natural luster.
  7. Painting & Finishing: After polishing, the idol is painted with vibrant colors that further enhance its beauty and detailing.
  8. Packaging & Transport: Finally, the finished idol is carefully packaged to ensure it reaches you safely and without any damage. We take great care in arranging secure transportation.
